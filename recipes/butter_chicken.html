Butter Chicken

Description:

Tender and juicy chicken thanks to a deliciously spiced yogurt marinade, Butter Chicken is ridiculously easy to make. The curry sauce is out of this world!

Here are the following ingredients to make this recipe.

Ingredients to marinate chicken:

  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garlic paste
  • Oil
  • Yogurt

How to marinate chicken:

  1. Pat chicken dry, add chilli powder, salt and lemon juice. Mix well and let ir rest.
  2. Add ginger, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, paprika, turmeric and greek yogurt. Let it marinate for at least 12 hours.

Ingredients for the sauce of the butter chicken:

  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamoms
  • Cloves
  • Garlic paste
  • Green chillies
  • Paprika
  • Garam masala
  • Cumin powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Kasuri Methi

How to make the butter chicken sauce:

  1. Add to the blender tomatoes, 28 cashews and half a cup of water. Grind until it looks like a smooth puree.
  2. Now into the pan add buttern cinnamon, cloves and cardamoms. Once they begin to sizzle add garlic paste, green chillies and fry on medium heat until smell goes away.
  3. Do not burn anything
  4. Turn off the heat stir red chilli powder, garam masala, cumin and coriander.
  5. Strain the tomato puree into the pan. Let it boil in medium heat.
  6. Final step is to cook the chicken in a different pan and then add in the pan that has the sauce.
